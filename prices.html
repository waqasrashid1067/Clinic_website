How graft pricing works

Most transplants are priced per graft, so the honest answer to “how much?” is “how many?” A receding hairline is usually 1,500–2,500 grafts. A hairline plus mid-scalp is usually 2,500–3,500. Full crown coverage can pass 4,000 and may be better split across two sessions a year apart.

Anyone quoting you a graft count over the phone, before seeing your scalp, is guessing. We will not do that.

Included in every hair transplant price

  • Consultation, examination and clinical photographs
  • All medication given on the day, and the take-home pack
  • The day-three wash, done for you at the clinic
  • Reviews at month 1, month 6 and month 12, with photographs
  • Corrective work on anything we placed, within the first year

Not included: travel, accommodation, and any medication you continue at home beyond the first month.

Paying

Cash, bank transfer and card are accepted. Treatments above a certain value can be split across three to six months at no extra cost — ask at the consultation and the schedule is written into your plan rather than agreed verbally.
